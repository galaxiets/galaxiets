  • 100kW Bidirectional Convertor

Features :


1.   400VAC 3phase 50Hz to (600VDC-1000VDC) bidirectional.
2.   SiC MOSFET based 100kW AC to DC and DC to AC bidirectional converter
3.   High power density with small 19’ 6U rack
4.   Overall Efficiency Up to 96%
5.   AC current and Voltage harmonics less then 3%
6.   V to G and G to V with wireless monitor and control
7.   Hot swappable technology
8.   Parallel up to n numbers to increase power rating
9.   CAN and RS485 communication in between modules
