OUR DESIGN PROCESS


REQUIREMENT GATHERING
We start by understanding your project requirements and specifications.
DESIGN CONCEPTUALIZATION
Our team develops multiple design concepts based on the requirements and selects the most optimal one.
DESIGN SIMULATION AND ANALYSIS
We use simulation tools such as SPICE, MATLAB, and Simulink to simulate and analyze the design for performance, efficiency, and reliability.
PCB DESIGN AND LAYOUT
Once the design is finalized, we create schematics, PCB layouts, and BOMs for the design.
PROTOTYPING AND TESTING
We create prototypes and perform testing to validate the design for performance, efficiency, and reliability.
DOCUMENTATION
We provide detailed documentation for the design, including schematics, PCB layouts, BOMs, and testing results.
