MECHANICAL DESIGN


Mechanical design is to design parts, components, products, or systems of mechanical nature. For example, designs of various machine elements such as shafts, bearings, clutches, gears, and fasteners fall into the scope of mechanical design.

OUR DESIGN PROCESS

Our mechanical design process is a comprehensive and collaborative approach that involves several steps to ensure that we deliver the best possible outcome for our clients. Here's a breakdown of our mechanical design

Requirement Specification
The first step is to gather all the necessary requirements from our clients. We take the time to understand your needs, expectations, and constraints before we start working on your project.
Concept Design
After we have a clear understanding of your requirements, we start developing the concept design. We use various tools and techniques to create 2D and 3D models of the proposed design.
Detailed Design
Once the concept design is approved, we move on to the detailed design phase. We create detailed 3D models, analyze the design using various software tools, and perform simulations to ensure the design meets all the requirements.
Design Review
Before we finalize the design, we conduct a thorough design review. This involves reviewing the design with our team of experts and discussing any concerns or issues that may arise.
Design Validation
Design, develop and manufacture new concepts and test the feasibility and workability of the same. Develop prototype and carry out various tests on the same, continuously changing the design as per the test result until the optimum is achieved.
Documentation
We provide comprehensive documentation that includes drawings, schematics, bills of materials, and assembly instructions. This documentation ensures that our clients have all the necessary information to manufacture or assemble the product.
Maintenance and Support
We provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure that the product continues to perform as expected.
